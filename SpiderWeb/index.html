ABOUT THE EVENT



Thiagarajar College of Engineering proudly present "CODE BEE" Hackathon. A 36-hour-long hackathon where thousands of students from all over India come together and work on a variety of problem statements. Problem statements include both software and electronic tracks. This contest brings you a golden opportunity to test your skills against the best of the best contenders for cash prizes, goodies, and more! Participants will be building projects from scratch, which will be presented to our panel of experienced judges to decide our winners finally. Join us and use this opportunity to think out of the box!





FAQs

What is the "CODE BEE" Hackathon ?

"CODE BEE" Hackathon is a 36-hour long weekend event where thousands of students from all over India collaborate to work on fun problem statements based on software and electronics domains to give an obliging solution.

Who can participate?

"CODE BEE" Hackathon is open to all undergraduate students from all colleges across India.

How much is the participation fee?

Participation is absolutely free inclusive of resources.

What are the prerequisites needed to participate in the hackathon?

No prerequisites are needed. Even a novice can excel in this event.

I have never done Tech projects before.. Can I still participate?

That's totally fine! We will be sharing with you all the resources required. It's never too late to start.

What is team size to participate in the hackathon?

You can participate individually or in teams of 2 or 3. If you are participating in the hackathon individually and looking for a team, we will help you in connecting to other individual participants to get you a team.

Will the hackathon be in person or online this year?

This year the "CODE BEE" hackathon will be conducted in complete online mode.
